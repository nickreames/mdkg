# Context

The public model needs clearer visual explanation, but launch-ready diagrams should remain deterministic, accessible, and maintainable.

# Contract

- Diagrams are implemented as accessible HTML/CSS/SVG first.
- Generated imagery, video, or high-production visual assets are deferred unless explicitly rescoped.
- Creative Production is a review/direction gate for hierarchy, visual rhythm, and public clarity.
- Diagram text must not duplicate inaccessible image-only copy.

# Acceptance

`task-559` records Creative Production review evidence, and `test-277`/`test-279` verify the resulting diagram and review proof.
